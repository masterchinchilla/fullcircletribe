Full Circle Tribe Bring the Good Full Circle

Full Circle Tribe sells handcrafted gifts made by artisans trained in traditional techniques. We specialize in authentic Native American textiles and jewelry, in particular from the Blackfoot tribe based in Ontario Canada.

Selling crafts brings opportunities onto Native American reservations, connects communities and preserves the proud history of our First Nations. Your purchase directly aids this cause and brings the wheel of generosity Full-Circle.
